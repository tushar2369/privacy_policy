Privacy Policy

Smart PDF Reader

Last updated: February 2026

Smart PDF Reader respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Smart PDF Reader does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or contacts.

File Access

The app requires storage/media access permissions only to allow you to:

  • Open PDF files from your device
  • Read and display PDF documents
  • Share PDF files when you choose

All files remain on your device. We do not upload or store your files on any external server.

Advertising

Smart PDF Reader uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect anonymous data such as advertising ID (AD_ID), device type, and usage statistics to serve relevant ads.

We do not have access to or control over the data collected by Google. Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for more details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Smart PDF Reader is not specifically targeted at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Since no personal data is collected or stored, the risk is minimal.
