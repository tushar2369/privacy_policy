Frostit ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Photo Resizer & Compressor mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information Collection and Use

While using our application, we may require certain permissions to provide a functional and seamless experience.

Media and Storage Access

Our app requires access to your device's storage and media library (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_IMAGES) to:

  • Select photos for resizing or compression.
  • Save the processed images back to your device.

We do not upload your personal photos to any external servers. All processing happens locally on your device.

Advertisements

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. To show relevant ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect:

  • Device ID & Advertising ID: (com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID) used for personalized advertising and analytics.
  • Network State: (ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, INTERNET) to fetch ad content.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

4. Children’s Privacy
